Features

Practice Test

On Point offers users extensive WorkKeys practice tests. Each question is multiple choice and gives a thorough explanation of the reasoning used to arrive at the correct answer.

Contact a Representative

From the On Point application, you can contact a representative to schedule a test. Methods of contact include by phone or by filling out a form.

Locate a Test Facility

Official WorkKeys exams may be taken at a test facility. Upon location selection, Google maps opens and give directions to the user.

On Point developed in 2016 as a senior project for the course CS 426 at the University of Nevada, Reno.
