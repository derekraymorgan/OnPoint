Features of On Point

About

A major priority for On Point is to supply users with a more information about the ACT NCRC. Flipping to the next page is as easy as swiping your finger on the screen.

Frequently Asked Questions

After knowing more about the ACT NCRC you will surley have more questions about it, this is the place to get the answers you need.

Customized Practice Tests

Only practice the subject in which you need to improve on or only practice at a particular skill level.

Multiple Choice

The practice test questions are multiple choice just like the real test. Each answer is thoroughly explained so that users can gain a better understanding.

Real World

The test questions are very practical, real world questions that help users understand their strengths and weaknesses.

Contact Methods

The application provides multiple ways to contact an On Point representative.

Facility Locator

Find the nearest official ACT NCRC testing facility, then get directions to this facility with a simple click.

Resources

There are many resources about the ACT NCRC. Check these resources out to learn even more about it.

Testimonials

On Point offers testimonials from manufacturing leaders about the success they have had with the ACT NCRC.

On Point developed in 2016 as a senior project for the course CS 426 at the University of Nevada, Reno.
